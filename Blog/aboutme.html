ABOUT ME

Hi! I´m Avril and I´m 18 years old. I´m an argentinian college student currently studing engineering in my country. But now let´s talk about that we have in common: BOOKS.

I started reading when I was 12 with a saga that you know for sure: Harry Potter, but it wasn´t till 2021 that I started to read what, for me, is a lot. Before that, I used to read 4/5 per year, but during 2021 I read 20!!!

I`m a HUGE fan of fantasy, young adult and crime storys, for me they are superior!! And, althought I would love to love clasics, they are incredibly difficult for me to read (I think that they need to have some kind of fantasy to be appeling to me).

My favourite things in life are books, music, films, autumn, candles, traveling and the smell of rain and old books.

Last but not least, my biggest dream is to move to Europe and live in a small village in Italy or Edinburgh amd open a beautiful Dark Academia library.
